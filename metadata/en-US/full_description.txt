OpenBahn Navigator is a privacy-friendly, open-source journey planner for German railways.

• Search connections without an account or API key
• Filter by transport mode, bike, Deutschland-Ticket, transfer time, and more
• Station departure and arrival boards
• ML-based connection predictions (Bahn-Vorhersage)
• Track journeys and get delay notifications
• Import tickets including Deutschland-Ticket with photo

Uses public bahn.de APIs. English and German supported.
